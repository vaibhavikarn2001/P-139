AI Ping Pong

Instructions

1.First Keep your laptop screen straight.
2.Move yourself approximately 2-4 feet away form the laptop.

3.Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
4.Now press the play button and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
5.This red paddel is your paddel that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
